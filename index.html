Machine Learning Fitness Tracker

Discover the intersection of technology and fitness through my work on a Machine Learning Fitness Tracker, a highlight of my project portfolio. This project is capable or taking accelerometer and gyroscope data and using it for innovative health and fitness solutions. By employing feature engineering and predictive modeling, including techniques like Fourier transformation and random forest algorithms, I have created a way to succesfully track various barbell exercises.
